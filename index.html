About Us

Welcome to Laps and Livres,and we are Nerdy

we are a queer-friendly swim group of nerds who pretend to be keeping fit in the water, but mostly meet for vibes, lunch and unpacking the latest drama in literary scenes.

Fun Times

Swim Meets

We hold a swim meet fortnightly, where we commandeer a section of the pool, dip our feet in and stealthly judge actual swimmers!

Brunches

Occassionally, we hop over to our favorite restaurant, order big ol platters and chat. Other times, it's just lots of feelings peeled off on the table.
